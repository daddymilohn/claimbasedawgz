How to buy

Step 1

Connect wallet

Head to the top of this page to find the presale widget. Connect your wallet from a variety of available options to get started. If you are not able to connect, please reach out to us on our official Telegram channel.

Step 2

Select Network

After connecting your wallet, you can opt to join the Base Dawgz presale on your choice of Base, Solana, Ethereum, BSC, or Avalanche chains. Buy either with native currency, or with USDT on Ethereum, bUSD on BSC and USDC on Base.

Step 3

Buy Tokens

Specify the amount of SOL, ETH, BNB, AVAX, USDT, or USDC you’d like to trade for $DAWGZ, and then approve the transaction in your wallet. During presale you will not receive tokens immediately, but will claim them after the presale.
